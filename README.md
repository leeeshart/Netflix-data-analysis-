#  Netflix Data Analysis

##  Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover patterns, trends, and insights about the platform's content.

---

##  Objective
The goal of this project is to:
- Analyze the distribution of Movies vs TV Shows  
- Identify top countries producing Netflix content  
- Understand content growth over time  
- Discover the most popular genres  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  

---

##  Data Cleaning
- Handled missing values in columns like country, director, and cast  
- Removed duplicate records  
- Converted `date_added` to datetime format  
- Extracted year from date for trend analysis  

---

##  Analysis Performed

###  Content Type Distribution
- Compared Movies and TV Shows on Netflix  

###  Country-wise Analysis
- Identified countries contributing the most content  

###  Trend Analysis
- Analyzed how Netflix content has grown over the years  

###  Genre Analysis
- Split and analyzed multiple genres using Pandas  
- Identified the top 10 most common genres  

---

##  Key Insights
- Movies dominate Netflix content, indicating a focus on scalable formats  
- The United States contributes the largest share of content  
- Netflix content increased significantly after 2015  
- Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres  

---

##  Business Insight
The dominance of movies suggests that Netflix prioritizes content that is easier to produce and distribute globally compared to long-running TV series.
